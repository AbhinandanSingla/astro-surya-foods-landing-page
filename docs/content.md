### Our Process

| Step | Title | Description |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Paddy Procurement | Direct sourcing from trusted farmer networks across India's premier rice belt regions, ensuring traceability from field to factory. |
| 2 | Milling & Husking | State-of-the-art rice milling equipment engineered for minimal grain breakage and maximum yield, preserving the integrity of every grain. |
| 3 | Sortex & Grading | Multi-pass color sorting and precision length grading technology ensures uniformity in grain size, color, and appearance across every batch. |
| 4 | Quality Lab Testing | In-house laboratory testing for moisture content, grain length, aroma index, broken grain percentage, and foreign matter — every batch, every time. |
| 5 | Packaging | Hygienic, automated packaging lines for both consumer retail formats (1kg–10kg) and bulk export formats (25kg–50kg) with nitrogen flushing for freshness. |
| 6 | Export Compliance | Full fumigation, phytosanitary certification, certificates of origin, commercial invoicing, and customs documentation for hassle-free international trade. |
